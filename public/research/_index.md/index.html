Academic Research#

Geographic Concentration in Global Semiconductor Manufacturing#

Python, SQL | 2025-2026

Analysed the $500B global semiconductor market to assess supply chain concentration risk for corporate and government decision-makers.

Key Findings:

  • Despite $95B in government subsidies, market concentration increased
  • Taiwan’s 31% market share represents an irreplaceable ecosystem
  • US manufacturing costs 56% more than Taiwan equivalents
  • Ecosystem factors (talent, suppliers, infrastructure) matter more than subsidies
