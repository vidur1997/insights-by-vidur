How I Approach Market Sizing

2026-02-15

Market sizing is one of the most fundamental consulting skills. Here is my approach.

The Framework

I typically use a combination of top-down and bottom-up approaches.

Top-Down

Start with the total addressable market and narrow down:

Total Market then Segment then Geography then Target Customers

Bottom-Up

Build up from unit economics:

Price x Volume x Customers = Market Size

Key Principles

  1. Triangulate - Use multiple methods and cross-check
  2. Document assumptions - Make them explicit and defensible
  3. Sensitivity analysis - Test what happens if you are wrong
